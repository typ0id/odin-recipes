Egg Muffin Sandwich

Description

A quick and easy brunch recipe! A simple sandwich mostly customizable to your liking. It's filing and nutritious.

This is great for when you need a quick pick-me-up and just want a hearty sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Egg muffins
  • A meat of your choosing (I like to choose spam, a sausage patty, or even deli meat!)
  • 1 large egg
  • A green of your choosing (Again, I like lettuce or spinach as a green.
  • Salt and pepper
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Cheese of your choosing (If you need some recommendations, go with american or swiss cheese.)
  • An oil of your choosing (This can be coconut oil, butter, vegetable oil. I highly enjoy coconut oil!)

Steps

  1. In a small pan, add oil to pan. Set heat to medium-low.
  2. Once the pan has heated, add in the egg muffin sides and fry. Remove once fried and set aside.
  3. Add in more oil if needed, and add in the egg. Cook however you like your eggs done. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add in your meat of choice and cook completely. Set on top of the bottom of the egg muffin.
  5. Assemble your sandwich however you like. Drizzle on some kewpie mayo and add your cheese.
  6. Done!
